Drag and Drop Interface


Use the mobile survey builder to build dynamic surveys, without any programming knowledge needed. Select from a growing and extensive list of unique field types, customizable to your specific needs.

Enable conditions to incorporate branching logic and data validation. You can even go a step further and customize the background and font color of your fields to improve adherence from your participants.





Limitless Branching Logic


Create complex branching logic with arithmetic and logical rules. Nest rules, link different fields and achieve a world-class mobile survey experience.




Team Management


As your team scales, you want to make sure you are using the best practices for controlling access to your study.

We've built an entirely customizable permissions manager that allows you to give pre-defined functions such as View All, Add Entry, Edit Entry or Export Data to individual users or groups that you create.





Automatic Calculations


Automate arithmetic calculations within your surveys. Help your survey participants report more insightful information by giving them on-the-fly calculations that support them while doing data collection.




Build Visually Rich Graphs


Use our web application to generate graphs and monitor them over time. These visuals can be exported at any moment to PDF, JPEG, and PNG so you can use them in publications or presentations.

These custom graph dashboards allow you to auto-generate them into push reports that can be automatically emailed to specific individuals in your research teams on a recurring schedule.

The current graphs you can create with Teamscope are line, bar, histogram, radar and pie. More graph types are soon to come.
