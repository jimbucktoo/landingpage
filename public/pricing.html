Frequently Asked Questions

What is a user?

A user is someone with a Survaid survey conductory user account that accesses the platform to manage a project, build forms or simply collect data.

What is a survey?

A survey is a study that you build on Survaid. A survey has its own users and fields and it can be a clinical study, a monitoring and evaluation project or an agricultural development initiative.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You may cancel your subscription by requesting it via email to support@survaidapp.com, with a minimum notice period of 7 days. Or you may visit your account settings, scroll all the way down to the bottom of "Overview", and select "Cancel Subscription".

Can I try Survaid before committing to a plan?

Of course! Schedule a demo to access our free, 30-day trial of our standard package, no strings attached. After your free trial expires you can choose a plan or if Survaid wasn't the right platform for you, simply cancel your subscription and take your data with you.

Is Survaid secure and HIPAA/GCP Complaint?

Yes, Survaid is designed for sensitive and medical data collection. Our platform encrypts data storage and transmission. You may track all data creation and edits with our audit trail and control your peer's access with our permissions manager. Survaid complies with Good Clinical Practice (GCP) and HIPAA.

How is the data stored?

Your data is stored with AES-256 encryption in servers in Seattle, Washington by Amazon Web Services. AWS complies with ISO 27001 and ISO 9001. All data is backed up daily for long-term retention. Survaid employees do not have access to your data and only upon your request may we decrypt it.

What happens to my data after my study finishes?

After you cancel your subscription with Survaid, or your project finishes, your data will still be stored for 1 year. You may log back in at any moment during that period and export your data.
