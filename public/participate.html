What's Survaid?

Survaid is a mobile and web application platform that allows users to participate in research studies virtually using everyday smartphones.

Survey participants can earn money by completing surveys that utilize a variety of sensors built into your smartphone that tracks dynamic metrics to be processed for research data.

Whether you are conducting a clinical trial at a hospital or examining patients in a remote village, our applications allow you to work wherever you need. Our mobile apps are offline-friendly which means data can be stored without the need for internet and synchronized once you gain a connection.

You can download Survaid on your iPhone, iPad and Android device or login with our web app.





Powerful, Offline Surveys

Participate in powerful, offline surveys that can harness the power of built-in mobile phone sensors to create multi-dimensional data visualizations. Contribute to cutting edge research studies and earn money at an hourly rate.

With offline data collection, participants can securely capture data anywhere, anytime, without the need for internet connectivity. Once the participant can connect to the internet again, they can simply upload the data to our cloud server.

Filter through surveys based upon their pay rate, category, location, time posted, and a variety of other filters.

View detailed comments left on surveys by previous participants' to find the right fit for you.

If a survey finds your interest, fill out the survey application form to add your spot in the "Partipant Queue".





Real-Time Messaging
& Push Notifications

Survaid offers secure, real-time messaging and personalized push notifications to provide better communication with your fellow researchers and participants.

Survaid is secure by default because we built end-to-end encryption into the latest versions of our app so that you'll get fast, simple, secure messaging that exceeds requirements for HIPAA compliance.

We've also developed the ability for you to define fully customizable events. Whenever an event is triggered, you can instruct the platform to send a message to specific researchers and participants on your team and alert them with a custom push notification.





Build Your Brand

Fully customizable personal profile to market yourself to survey conductors and rank higher in the Applicant Queue. The Applicant Queue is the list of applicants who have applied for any given survey in which the survey conductors will choose the survey partipants from.

Display your uniqueness by writing a detailed bio in our about section to tell survey conductors who you are. Bio descriptions with higher character counts tend to list higher in the Applicant Queue.

Showcase surveys you have previously participated in. If you specialize in a certain category of research participation, you will be more attractive to research conductors and rank higher in the Applicant Queue.

Survey conductors may also leave ratings and reviews on your profile to share feedback from your past survey performances. Your participant feedback rating will also factor in to your Applicant Queue ranking.
